Daniel Otto

Daniel playing an acoustic guitar

Dan has been in love with music from a young age. At age 9, he began playing the viola and has had a love for all string instruments ever since. He continued his viola performance and music studies into his undergraduate degree, but not before his greater love for music made him broaden his instrumental horizons, picking up the guitar and drums.
His musical influences vary as greatly as the music he listens to. Everything from classic rock, to surf, to pop, to punk, to hip hop is fair game. It’s quite hard to find a genre in which Dan cannot find music to like. An avid Streetlight Manifesto fan, ska punk has always had a huge impact on him musically. Other musical influences include The Smashing Pumpkins, The Strokes, Brand New, Blink-182, Radiohead, LCD Soundsystem, PUP, FIDLAR, Sublime, Linkin Park, and more.
Dan’s primary concern is to make music that the audience can feel involved with. If Dan writes a song and feels like nobody can move around, dance, and sing along to it, that song goes into the scrap pile to get reworked.

Alek

Alek seen with his Höfner bass and weird face

Alexander Soerensen, or simply Alek as prefered, is the bands bass-player, social-media responsible and overall nerd.
As a young boy, he fell in love with lots of different bands, such as The Beatles, Greenday, Rolling stones and Muse. Being inspired, Alek wanted to take guitar-lessons, but his parents insisted that he should learn to play the piano instead. After two years of playing the piano without improvements and lack of motivation, Alek finally was allowed to take guitar-lessons. After playing for a couple of years, Alek eventually bought himself a low-budget Fender P-bass. Initially, not much effort was put into the instrument, but after a couple of years Alek started practising and built up a decent amount of skills on the bass. He played at a couple different occasions, but it was first when he joined "here be dragons!" that he really focused on the bass.
